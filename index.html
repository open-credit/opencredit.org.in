The Problem

63 million Indian MSMEs contribute 30% to GDP but only 14% have access to formal credit. Traditional scoring is opaque, biased, and excludes the informal sector.

The Solution: OpenCredit

An open-source credit scoring engine that uses UPI transaction history to provide transparent, fair, and instant credit scores — visible to borrowers before they apply.
