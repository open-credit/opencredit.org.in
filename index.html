Executive Summary

Despite UPI processing ₹23.5 trillion monthly, traditional credit scoring remains opaque, biased, and excludes millions of honest merchants.

OpenCredit is an open-source credit assessment engine that uses UPI transaction history to generate transparent, auditable credit scores — visible to borrowers before they apply.

Core Principles

  • 100% open-source scoring algorithm (Apache 2.0)
  • UPI transaction data as primary signal
  • Mandatory disclosure of all lender adjustments
  • Multi-lender competitive marketplace
  • Borrower empowerment: see your score first
  • Full compliance with DPDP Act 2023 & RBI guidelines

Projected 5-Year Impact

  • 50 million merchants gain transparent credit scores
  • ₹5 trillion additional credit to underserved segments
  • 40% reduction in rejection rates
  • 30% lower borrowing costs via competition
